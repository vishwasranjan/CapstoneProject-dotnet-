How to download ITR Forms(PDF (For ITR 1/2/4s) / XML (For ITR 3) Format) ?

Step 1. Visit www.incometaxindiaefilling.gov.in, click on the "Login Here" button and insert your credentials to log in to your Income Tax account.


Step 2. Click ont the "My Accounts - View e-Filed Returns / Forms", Select Income Tax Returns in the dropbtndown provided and click on "Submit" button.


Step 3. Click on the number provided below the haeder "Ack. No."
Click on "ITR Form" for ITR 1/2/4 and "XML" For ITR 3 under the heading "Download/Status Description.


Step 4. Click on the "Back" button and download the "PDF/Xml" files by following Step-3 for the relevant.


Step 5. Upload the saved "PDF / XML" files on www.pcploansin59minutes.com
